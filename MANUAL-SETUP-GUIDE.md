# Manual Setup Guide for A6-9V
## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Sign in to Microsoft Account (keamouyleng@proton.me)

1. **Open Windows Settings**
   - Press `Windows Key + I`
   - Or click Start Menu > Settings (gear icon)

2. **Go to Accounts**
   - Click on "Accounts" in the left sidebar
   - Click on "Your info" tab

3. **Sign in to Microsoft Account**
   - Click "Sign in with a Microsoft account instead"
   - Enter: `keamouyleng@proton.me`
   - Enter your password
   - Follow the verification steps if prompted

4. **Enable Sync Settings**
   - Go to Settings > Accounts > Sync your settings
   - Turn ON "Sync settings"
   - Enable all the sync options you want:
     - Theme
     - Passwords
     - Language preferences
     - Ease of Access
     - Other Windows settings

---

### Step 2: Sign in to OneDrive

1. **Open OneDrive**
   - Click the OneDrive icon in the system tray (bottom right)
   - Or search for "OneDrive" in Start Menu

2. **Sign in**
   - Enter: `keamouyleng@proton.me`
   - Enter your password
   - Click "Sign in"

3. **Choose Folders to Sync**
   - OneDrive will ask which folders to sync
   - Select the folders you want to sync
   - Click "Next" and then "Open my OneDrive folder"

4. **Verify Sync**
   - Check the OneDrive icon in system tray
   - It should show a green checkmark when synced
   - Right-click the icon > Settings to configure sync options

---

### Step 3: Sign in to Google Drive

1. **Install Google Drive (if not installed)**
   - Go to: https://www.google.com/drive/download/
   - Download "Google Drive for desktop"
   - Install it

2. **Sign in to Google Drive**
   - Open Google Drive from Start Menu
   - Enter your Google account email
   - Enter your password
   - Follow the verification steps

3. **Configure Sync**
   - Choose which folders to sync
   - Set up your Google Drive folder location
   - Verify sync is working

---

### Step 4: Sign in to Dropbox (if using)

1. **Install Dropbox (if not installed)**
   - Go to: https://www.dropbox.com/downloading
   - Download and install Dropbox

2. **Sign in to Dropbox**
   - Open Dropbox from Start Menu
   - Sign in with your Dropbox account
   - Choose folders to sync

---

### Step 5: Configure Default Apps

1. **Open Default Apps Settings**
   - Press `Windows Key + I` to open Settings
   - Go to Apps > Default apps

2. **Set Default Apps**
   - **Email**: Click "Email" > Choose "Mail" or "Outlook"
   - **Web browser**: Click "Web browser" > Choose your preferred browser
   - **Music player**: Click "Music player" > Choose your preferred app
   - **Video player**: Click "Video player" > Choose your preferred app
   - **Photo viewer**: Click "Photo viewer" > Choose your preferred app
   - **PDF files**: Click "PDF" > Choose your preferred PDF reader

3. **Set Defaults by File Type**
   - Scroll down to "Choose defaults by file type"
   - Click on file extensions and choose your preferred apps

---

### Step 6: Enable Browser Sync

#### For Google Chrome:
1. Open Chrome
2. Click the profile icon (top right)
3. Click "Sign in to Chrome"
4. Enter your Google account email
5. Enter your password
6. Go to Settings (chrome://settings/)
7. Click "Sync and Google services"
8. Click "Manage what you sync"
9. Enable all sync options:
   - Apps
   - Autofill
   - Bookmarks
   - History
   - Passwords
   - Settings
   - Themes
   - Open tabs

#### For Microsoft Edge:
1. Open Edge
2. Click the profile icon (top right)
3. Click "Sign in to sync data"
4. Enter: `keamouyleng@proton.me`
5. Enter your password
6. Go to Settings (edge://settings/)
7. Click "Profiles" > "Sync"
8. Turn ON "Sync"
9. Enable all sync options

#### For Firefox:
1. Open Firefox
2. Click the menu (three lines, top right)
3. Click "Sign in to Sync"
4. Create a Firefox account or sign in
5. Enable sync for:
   - Bookmarks
   - History
   - Passwords
   - Open tabs
   - Add-ons
   - Preferences

---

### Step 7: Verify All Cloud Services Are Syncing

1. **Check OneDrive**
   - Look for OneDrive icon in system tray
   - Should show green checkmark when synced
   - Right-click > View sync status
   - Create a test file in OneDrive folder to verify

2. **Check Google Drive**
   - Look for Google Drive icon in system tray
   - Should show "Synced" status
   - Create a test file in Google Drive folder to verify

3. **Check Dropbox**
   - Look for Dropbox icon in system tray
   - Should show green checkmark when synced
   - Create a test file in Dropbox folder to verify

4. **Test Sync**
   - Create a test file in each cloud folder
   - Wait a few seconds
   - Check online (OneDrive.com, drive.google.com, dropbox.com)
   - Verify the files appear online

## Troubleshooting

### OneDrive Not Found
- OneDrive might be installed but not in the expected location
- Try: Settings > Apps > Search for "OneDrive"
- Or download from: https://www.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/onedrive/download

### Google Drive Not Found
- Download from: https://www.google.com/drive/download/
- Install "Google Drive for desktop"

### Dropbox Not Found
- Download from: https://www.dropbox.com/downloading

### Sync Not Working
- Check internet connection
- Verify you're signed in to each service
- Check Windows Security exclusions (should be configured by script)
- Restart the cloud service
- Check firewall settings
